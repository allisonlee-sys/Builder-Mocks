From deploy to fix in under 30 minutes

We shipped a checkout redesign. Here's exactly what happened next.

What happened
Checkout conversion crashed 51% after the release
  • Revenue metrics turned red within the hour
  • No one on the team knew why
  • Guardrail alert fired, rollout paused
→
What Amplitude showed
Exactly where users dropped off and why
  • 73% drop between CTA click and checkout start
  • Control group: only 38% at this step
  • Session replays: address form below fold on mobile
→
What happened next
Conversion recovered in 20 minutes
  • Flag rolled back while scroll fix was PR'd
  • Fix shipped, flag re-enabled
  • Conversion back to 14.2% baseline
